Key points
  1. In order to fulfil your potential, focus on your strengths.
  2. Manage your weaknesses, but only to the point where they do not prevent you from exercising your strengths.
  3. Most of us do not put our strengths to use. As a result, we do not fulfil our potential for success or happiness.
  4. Sometimes our strengths are not apparent; they may be hidden behind our weaknesses.
  5. We will go through a process of discovery and development to uncover these strengths.
